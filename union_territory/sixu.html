popular places in ladakh
Leh: largest town and the capital of Ladakh, Leh is known for its historic monasteries, vibrant markets, and the Leh Palace. It serves as a base for many travelers exploring the region
Pangong Lake:- This high-altitude lake is renowned for its clear blue waters and breathtaking scenery. The lake extends from India to China, and its changing colors throughout the day make it a must-visit destination.

Additional Place in ladakh for visit

Nubra Valley: for its unique landscapes, Nubra Valley is famous for the Hunder Sand Dunes, where visitors can experience a desert-like environment amidst the high mountains. The valley is also home to Diskit Monastery.

Tso Moriri Lake: Another beautiful high-altitude lake in Ladakh, Tso Moriri is surrounded by mountains and offers a serene and peaceful environment. It's a great place for bird watching and enjoying the tranquility of nature.

Hemis Monastery: One of the largest and most famous monasteries in Ladakh, Hemis Monastery is known for its annual Hemis Festival, showcasing traditional dance and cultural performances.
