Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) was a union territory in northern India until October 31, 2019, when it was reorganized into two separate union territories: Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh.
popular places in Jammu & Kashmir
Srinagar : is a city in the Indian-administered Jammu and Kashmir union territory. It is the summer capital of the union territory, with Jammu as the winter capital.
Gulmarg:- Known for its picturesque landscapes, Gulmarg is a popular hill station and skiing destination. The Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, offers breathtaking views of the snow-clad mountains

Additional Place in Jammu & Kashmir for visit

Pahalgam: A serene town located on the banks of the Lidder River, Pahalgam is known for its lush meadows and is a starting point for the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage. Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari are some of the attractions in the vicinity.

Jammu: The winter capital of Jammu & Kashmir, Jammu is known for its historic temples, including the famous Vaishno Devi shrine. The Raghunath Temple, Ranbireshwar Temple, and Mubarak Mandi Palace are some of the key attractions in the city.

Leh-Ladakh: Although Ladakh is a separate union territory, it shares historical and cultural ties with Jammu & Kashmir.
