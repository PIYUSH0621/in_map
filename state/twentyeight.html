West Bengal is an Indian state which lies in the eastern region of India. It is the fourth-most populous state of India, having over 91 million citizens. The state has a rural population of about 60%, and most of its villagers live in small hamlets.
* Dakshineswar Kali Temple : Dakshineswar Kali Temple is a Hindu navaratna temple in Dakshineswar, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Situated on the eastern bank of the Hooghly River, the presiding deity of the temple is Bhavatarini, a form of Parashakti Adya Kali, otherwise known as Adishakti Kalika.
