*Namdapha National Park: This is the largest protected area in the Eastern Himalaya and is known for its diverse flora and fauna, including the elusive snow leopard

* Tawang Monastery: in Tawang district, this is the largest monastery in India and an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists. It offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. .


Additional Place in Arunachal Pradesh

Sela Pass: Situated at an elevation of about 13,700 feet, Sela Pass is known for its picturesque landscape, especially the Sela Lake, which remains frozen in winter.

Ziro Valley: Famous for its lush landscapes, rice fields, and the unique Apatani tribal culture, Ziro Valley is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Bomdila: It is a charming town known for its monasteries, apple orchards, and the Bomdila Monastery, which offers panoramic views of the Himalayan landscapes.
