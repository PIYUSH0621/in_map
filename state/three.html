Goa is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage. It is the smallest state in India by area and the fourth smallest by population . Goa was a Portuguese colony until 1961 when it was annexed by India
* baga beach :Baga Beach is one of the most famous and lively beaches in Goa. It offers a combination of water sports, beach shacks, and a vibrant nightlife scene.

* Mangueshi Temple : The Mangeshi Temple, also known as Mangueshi Temple, is one of the most prominent and revered temples in Goa, India. Dedicated to Lord Mangesh .


Additional Place in goa

* Dudhsagar Waterfalls: Located in the Mollem National Park, Dudhsagar Waterfalls is a stunning natural attraction.

* Aguada Fort : Perched on a hilltop overlooking the Arabian Sea, Aguada Fort offers panoramic views and a glimpse into Goa's historical past.

* Old Goa: For a glimpse into the state's rich history and colonial past, Old Goa is a UNESCO World Heritage Site with well-preserved churches and cathedrals.
