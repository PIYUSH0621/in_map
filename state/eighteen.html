Mizoram Known for its evergreen hills and dense bamboo jungles, Mizoram lies in the southern most tip of north east India. Called the Land of Blue Mountains, the hills are crisscrossed by gushing rivers and high sparkling waterfalls.
* Palak Lake : Palak Lake, also known as Palak Dil, is the largest lake in Mizoram. It is located near Phura village and is surrounded by lush greenery. The lake is known for its tranquility and is a popular spot for boating.


Additional Place in Mizoram

* Phawngpui : Known as the "Blue Mountain," Phawngpui is the highest peak in Mizoram. It is a trekker's paradise, offering stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

* Aizawl : The capital city of Mizoram, Aizawl, is known for its vibrant culture, markets, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

* Champhai : Champhai, situated near the Indo-Myanmar border, is known for its scenic beauty.

* Dampa Tiger Reserve : Located in the western part of Mizoram, It is home to various species of flora and fauna, including the Bengal tiger.
