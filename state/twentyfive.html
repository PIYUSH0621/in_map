Telangana is the 29th state of India, formed on the 2nd of June 2014. The state has an area of 1,14,840 Sq Km and has a population of 3,52,86,757. The Telangana region was part of the Hyderabad state from Sept 17th 1948 to Nov 1st 1956, until it was merged with Andhra state to form the Andhra Pradesh state.
* Ramoji film city: Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio facility located in Abdullahpurmet, Hyderabad. Spread over 1,666 acres (674 ha), it is the largest film studio complex in the world and as such has been certified by the Guinness World Records. It was established by Telugu media tycoon Ramoji Rao in 1996.

* charminar : The four pillars around Charminar represent the four caliphs. These pillars or minars are 48.7 meters high. These are four stories, with each floor divided by intricately carved rings that surround it.


Additional Place in Telangana

* Warangal Fort: The Warangal Fort, also known as the Kakatiya Kala Thoranam, is a historical fort in Warangal.

* Golkonda Fort : Located in Hyderabad, Golkonda Fort is known for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

* Hussain Sagar Lake: An artificial lake built by Hazrat Hussain Shah Wali in 1562, Hussain Sagar Lake is one of the largest man-made lakes in Asia.
