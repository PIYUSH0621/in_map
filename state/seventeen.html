Located in North-east India, Meghalaya, also known as the Abode of Clouds, falls in one of the richest biodiversity areas in the world. The famed monsoons, the rich traditional festivals, and the dramatic terrain are just some of the many things this state is famous for. Whether you are looking for an adventure to give you an adrenaline high or a tranquil spot to recharge yourself, Meghalaya has plenty to offer.
* Shillong :The capital city of Meghalaya, Shillong is known for its pleasant climate,beautiful landscapes,and vibrant culture. Some popular attractions in Shillong include Ward's Lake, Shillong Peak, and the Don Bosco Centre for Indigenous Cultures.


Additional Place in Meghalaya

* Living Root Bridges :its unique living root bridges in the villages of Mawlynnong and Cherrapunji. are created by guiding the roots of rubber trees over rivers.

* Dawki : Located near the Bangladesh border, is also known Umngot River, famous for its crystal-clear waters. and the annual boat race on the Umngot River are popular.

* Elephant Falls :Situated near Shillong, is a popular waterfall with three successive falls, and it is surrounded by lush greenery.

* Nongkhnum Island : This is the largest river island in Meghalaya, located on the Brahmaputra River.
