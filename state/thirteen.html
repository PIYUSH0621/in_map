Jharkhand is a state located in the eastern part of India. It was carved out of the southern part of Bihar in the year 2000 to become the 28th state of the Indian Union.
*Jamshedpur: Known as the "Steel City," Jamshedpur is an industrial hub. It houses Tata Steel and offers attractions like Jubilee Park, Dimna Lake, and the Tata Steel Zoological Park.

* Ranchi: The capital city of Jharkhand,Ranchi, is known for its scenic beauty with lakes and hills. Places of interest include Rock Garden, Tagore Hill, and Ranchi Lake


Additional Place in Jharkhand

Dhanbad: Recognized for its coal mines, Dhanbad is an important industrial city. It also has the Indian School of Mines and offers attractions like Topchanchi Lake and Shakti Mandir.

Hazaribagh: is known for its picturesque landscapes, wildlife sanctuary, and Hazaribagh National Park. The Hazaribagh Lake and Canary Hill are popular attractions.

Deoghar: for the Baidyanath Jyotirlinga Temple, Deoghar is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus.
