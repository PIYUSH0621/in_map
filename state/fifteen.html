Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a state located on the southwestern coast of India. Kerala is one of the small states of India in terms of area. it consists of only 1.3 per cent of the total area of India.
* Thiruvananthapuram : The capital city of Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram is known for its temples, museums, and the historic Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple.


Additional Place in kerala

* Alappuzha : Famous for its backwaters, houseboats, and intricate network of canals.

* Kochi : A vibrant city with a rich history and cultural heritage. Kochi is known for its historical sites.

* Munnar : A picturesque hill station known for its lush green tea plantations, misty hills, and serene landscapes.

* Kovalam : Kovalam is a great place for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports.
