Maharashtra, a diverse and dynamic state situated in the western part of India, boasts a rich historical tapestry that spans millennia. Home to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization and later a significant center during the Vedic period, Maharashtra witnessed the rise and fall of empires such as the Satavahanas and Rashtrakutas.
