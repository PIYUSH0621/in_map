Sikkim is notable for its biodiversity, including alpine and subtropical climates, as well as being a host to Kanchenjunga, the highest peak in India and third highest on Earth. Sikkim's capital and largest city is Gangtok.
* Gangtok : It has an amazing view of mount Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain peak in the world. Also like everything around it, Gangtok is abundant in natural beauty.

most languages used : Hindi,English, Nepali, Sikkimese, and Lepcha.

food : momos, Thukpa, and Gundruk.
* Tsomgo Lake: the lake remains frozen during the winter season. The lake surface reflects different colours with change of seasons and is held in great reverence by the local Sikkimese people.


Additional Place in Sikkim

* Nathula Pass: is one of the three open trading border posts between India and China, Nathu La Pass has played a key role in the Sino-Indian Trade.

* Pelling : Pelling also has sites for mountain biking, rock climbing, village tours, meditations, apart from serving as a base for a number of treks in the region.

* Teesta River:Teesta River is considered to be the lifeline of the state its entire length of 393 km (245 miles).
