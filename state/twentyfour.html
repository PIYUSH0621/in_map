Tamil Nadu is a state located in the southern part of India. It is the seventh-largest state in terms of area and the sixth-most populous state in the country. The capital of Tamil Nadu is Chennai, which is one of the major cultural, economic, and educational centers in South India.
* Meenakshi Amman Temple: The temple is considered to be one of the oldest and most significant temples in India, with a Madurai Meenakshi Amma Temple history that dates back more than 2500 years. According to legend, the temple was built by the Pandya king, Kulasekara Pandya, in the 6th century CE.

* Kanyakumari: Kanniyakumari is a popular tourist destination and pilgrimage centre in India. Notable tourist spots include its unique sunrise and sunset points, the 41-metre (133 ft) Thiruvalluvar Statue, and Vivekananda Rock Memorial off the coast.


Additional Place in Tamil Nadu

* Vellore fort : The fort houses the Jalakanteswarar Hindu temple, the Christian St. John's Church and a Muslim mosque, of which the Jalakanteswarar Temple is famous for its magnificent carvings.

* Rameshwaram Temple : Located in the centre of town, It is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to the deity Shiva.

* Kanchipuram : Kanchipuram is known for its temple architectures, 1000-pillared halls, huge temple towers and silk saris.
