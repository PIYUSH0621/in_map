manipur is Located in the northeast part of India, Manipur charms you with its simplicity and serenity. It is the land of rich valleys surrounded by beautiful hills and lakes, a land of gentle people full of laughter and joy.
* Shri Govindajee Temple : This temple, located in Imphal, is dedicated to Lord Krishna and is an important religious site in Manipur. It reflects the cultural and religious heritage of the region.


Additional Place in manipur

* Kangla Fort : Located in Imphal, Kangla Fort holds historical and cultural significance.

* Zeilad Lake : Zeilad Lake is located in the Churachandpur district. It is a serene lake surrounded by hills and forests.

* Thalon Cave : Thalon Cave is a limestone cave system located in the Tamenglong district. It is known for its stalactite and stalagmite formations

* Dzuko Valley : Dzuko Valley, located on the border of Manipur and Nagaland, is known for its stunning landscapes
